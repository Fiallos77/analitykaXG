### Match Context
This analysis focuses on Ecuador vs Argentina (2026), examining how Ecuador managed to neutralize the world champion’s build-up play and progress in key areas.

### Data & Metrics
- **Heatmaps**: shows team positioning and zones of influence  
- **xG (expected goals)**: evaluates quality of scoring chances  
- **Passing Networks**: highlights key connections and dangerous passes  

### Key Findings
1. Ecuador blocked central lanes effectively, forcing Argentina to play wide.  
2. High pressing moments limited Argentina’s ball progression.  
3. Heatmaps show Ecuador maintained compact blocks during defensive transitions.

### Coaching Insight
From a tactical perspective:
- Compact mid-blocks disrupted Argentina’s possession-based game.  
- Quick transitions from defense to attack created counter opportunities.
